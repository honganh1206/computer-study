For two bit vectors $\vec{x} = [x_{w-1}, x_{w-2}, \dots,x_{0}]$ of length $w$ and $\vec{x'} = [0,\dots,0,x_{w-1}, x_{w-2}, \dots,x_{0}]$ of length $w'$, where $w' > w$

$$
B 2 T_{w}(x) = B 2 T_{w}(x')
$$

**KEY IDEA:** The leading zeros do not add up to a value.
